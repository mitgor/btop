### Integration Gaps

**1. ThreadError handler is dead code** (TRANS-01, TRANS-03, EVENT-01)
Runner exception at `btop.cpp:541,719` writes `app_state.store(Error)` directly, never pushes `event::ThreadError` to queue. The `on_event(Running, ThreadError)` overload at line 333 is unreachable. Practical impact: low — app exits correctly via shadow atomic check in outer loop.

**2. RunnerStateVar is write-only** (RUNNER-01)
`runner_var` in the runner loop is assigned Idle/Collecting/Drawing but never read, visited, or dispatched. Shadow atomic `runner_state_tag` alone governs all cross-thread behavior. The variant provides no compile-time safety enforcement at runtime.

**3. Variant/shadow desync on thread error** (STATE-03, TRANS-03)
When runner stores `AppStateTag::Error` to shadow, `AppStateVar app_var` remains `state::Running`. `on_enter(state::Error)` never fires from this path. Compensated by `clean_quit()` printing error messages independently.
