### Integration Gaps (Accepted Tech Debt)

**1. ThreadError handler is dead code** (EVENT-03, TRANS-01)
Runner exception writes `app_state.store(Error)` directly, never pushes `event::ThreadError`. The `on_event(Running, ThreadError)` overload is unreachable at runtime. Practical impact: low — app handles errors via shadow atomic path.

**2. RunnerStateVar is write-only** (RUNNER-01)
`runner_var` in the runner loop is assigned Idle/Collecting/Drawing but never read. Shadow atomic `runner_state_tag` alone drives all behavior. The variant exists for future use.

**3. Variant/shadow desync on thread error** (STATE-03, TRANS-03)
When runner stores `AppStateTag::Error` to shadow, `app_var` remains `state::Running`. Compensated by `clean_quit()` handling error messages independently.
