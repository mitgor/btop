## Completed Phase Details

### Phase 28: Hot-Path POSIX I/O

- **PERF-04:** Cpu::collect and Proc::collect /proc/stat reads converted from ifstream to Tools::read_proc_file() with 32KB and 1KB stack buffers respectively. Zero heap allocation per collect cycle. String_view parsing with manual integer extraction.
- **PERF-05:** get_totalMem() and Mem::collect /proc/meminfo reads converted from ifstream to read_proc_file() with 256B and 4KB stack buffers. ZFS arcstats also converted. Line-start-aware matching prevents SwapCached/Cached ambiguity.
- **Verification:** 6/6 observable truths verified. Human-approved (Linux runtime testing deferred to Linux host).
- **Commits:** 4 implementation commits (8f9af53, 9cdc24f, 3de9643, 4eb0450)

## Gaps Analysis

### Unsatisfied Requirements

All 5 unsatisfied requirements are in unstarted phases — no plans, no code, no verification exists.

**Phase 29 (Draw Decomposition):**
- **DRAW-01:** Proc::draw() remains a monolithic function; no decomposition has been attempted

**Phase 30 (Unified Redraw):**

### Root Cause
